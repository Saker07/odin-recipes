Dirty Martini

Mix up a classic dirty martini to impress guests at your next party.
This easy, elegant cocktail combines dry gin, vermouth and green olives

Ingredients

  • 4-6 green olives, pitted
  • 25ml of the olives' brine
  • ice
  • 150ml London dry gin
  • 25ml dry vermouth

Steps

  1. Chill martini glass (fridge or crushed ice)
  2. Fill mixing glass with Ice, gin and olive brine
  3. Stir until the outside of the jug is cold
  4. Strain into the chilled glass
  5. Garnish with olive skewers
