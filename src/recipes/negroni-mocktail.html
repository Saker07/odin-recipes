Negroni Mocktail

Make an alcohol-free version of the classic negroni cocktail, with grape juice and a syrup made from grapefruit, coriander and cardamon pods

Ingredients

  • Syrup base
    • Â½ grapefruit (approx 125g)
    • 1 slice orange
    • 125g caster sugar
    • 3 cardamom pods, lightly crushed
    • pinch coriander seeds
    • few drops red food colouring
  • Cocktail
    • ice
    • 25ml white grape juice
    • 1 slice orange

Steps

  • Syrup
    1. Chop the grapefruit in small chunks
    2. Put the grapefruit chunks, the orange slice, sugar, 125ml water, chardamom pods and coriander seeds in a saucepan
    3. Heat the mixture until simmering and cook for around 5 mins, crushing the fruit pieces as it starts to soften to release the juices
    4. Once the fruit has softened and the white pith has faded, take it off the heat
    5. Leave to cool
    6. Add a splash of red food colouring to the syrup (optional)
  • Cocktail
    1. Strain the syrup
    2. Fill a tumbler with ice, 25ml syrup, 25ml cold water and the grape juice
    3. Stir gently until the outside of the tumbler feels cold
    4. Garnish with a slice of orange
